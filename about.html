What makes us different?

At Mark: MetaEarth, we are pushing the boundaries of what's possible with technology. Our platform is built on blockchain technology to allow transactions for users to place their digital assets in the real world via AR.

We are one of the very first applications to accurately pinpoint a location on vertical planes using sensor fusion methodologies and elevation APIs. This allows us to create a seamless and immersive experience for our users.

Meet the Team

Ziad Jarrar started his first tech start-up, Spot Media, in 2006, where they explored innovative and inspiring technologies such as projection mapping, AR, and other cutting-edge solutions at their very early stages.

In 2013, Google's announcement about Augmented Positioning Systems sparked an idea in Ziad Jarrar to create an AR social media experience. And so, the vision for Mark:MetaEarth was born.
